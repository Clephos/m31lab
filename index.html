About M31Lab

M31Lab LLC is a Puerto Rican startup founded in 2025, registered in Florida, inspired by the wonder and vast potential of the cosmos. Our name comes from Messier 31, the scientific designation for the Andromeda Galaxy—the nearest spiral galaxy to our own Milky Way and a symbol of exploration, scale, and infinite possibility.

We specialize in developing clean, intuitive, and purpose-driven iOS and web applications. At our core, we believe that great software should empower users without overwhelming them—tools that feel natural, beautiful, and responsive.

Born from a culture of resilience and creativity, M31Lab is committed to building digital experiences that reflect both technical excellence and a human touch.

Our Vision

We believe in empowering people through simple, purposeful design. Our goal is to make technology accessible and meaningful.

Past Projects

VentCalc.icu is a lightweight, web-based ventilator calculator designed for critical care professionals. Built for speed and clarity, it helps streamline key bedside calculations without the clutter of a full app. VentCalc.cc was created with responsiveness in mind and works seamlessly across devices—even in low-connectivity environments.

Current Projects

Pa' Kendo is our first iOS app, designed for absolute beginners interested in the art of kendo—the Japanese way of the sword. Whether you're just getting started or want a quick reference at your side, Pa' Kendo makes learning accessible, engaging, and fun.

Features:

  • Kendo Basics: A beginner-friendly guide to the art of kendo.
  • Glossary: Key kendo terms in kanji, romaji, English, and phonetic pronunciation.
  • Japanese Counting: Learn numbers using kanji, romaji, and Arabic numerals.
  • Quiz Mode: Test your knowledge with randomized quizzes covering vocabulary and numbers in multiple formats.
